Our Story

Two Businesses, One Roof

Don Chema's is part tienda, part deli. Walk in to shop for imported Central American and Mexican products you can't find at chain stores, then head to the counter in the back for a plate of fresh pupusas or baleadas made while you wait.

Located in the Doc Stone Retail center across from Home Depot on Garrisonville Rd. We open early for breakfast and keep the kitchen running until close. Family-run, family prices.

The Deli

Fresh pupusas, baleadas, tacos, tortas, and full breakfast plates made to order from scratch. The kitchen opens at 6 AM weekdays.

La Tienda

Imported groceries, seasonings, drinks, snacks, and specialty items from Honduras, El Salvador, Mexico, and Guatemala.
