Our Story

More Than a Deli

Don Chema's is a tienda and deli rolled into one. Walk in for groceries you can't find anywhere else in Stafford, imported straight from Central America and Mexico, then grab a plate of fresh pupusas or baleadas made to order while you shop.

Located in the Doc Stone Retail center across from Home Depot, we open at 6 AM for breakfast and serve lunch and dinner until 8 PM. Our kitchen runs on fresh ingredients, family recipes, and the kind of care that turns first-time visitors into regulars.
